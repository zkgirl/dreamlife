# DreamLife - Age Restrictions Guide

This document provides a comprehensive overview of all age restrictions implemented throughout the DreamLife game.

## Education System

### High School
- **Elementary School**: Auto-starts at age 6
- **Middle School**: Auto-starts at age 11
- **High School**: Auto-starts at age 14

### Higher Education
- **University**: Age 18+ (requires high school completion)
- **Graduate School**: Age 22+ (requires university completion)

## Implementation Notes

All age restrictions are enforced in the following ways:
1. **Visual feedback**: Color-coded indicators (green for eligible, red for restricted)
2. **Button states**: Disabled with cursor-not-allowed when restricted
3. **Alert messages**: User-friendly messages explaining requirements and countdown
4. **Purchase blocking**: Prevents transactions for age-restricted items
5. **Application blocking**: Prevents job applications for underage applicants
6. **Menu access**: Restricts access to education levels based on age and prerequisites

This ensures a realistic progression system where players experience age-appropriate content and opportunities throughout their virtual life.
